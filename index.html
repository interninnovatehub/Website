CCNA

CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) is a widely recognized certification in the field of networking. It validates a professional's ability to install, configure, operate, and troubleshoot small to medium-sized networks. CCNA-certified individuals possess foundational knowledge of networking concepts, protocols, and technologies, making them capable of managing and maintaining network infrastructures effectively.
